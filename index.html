About Me

I'm a Physics graduate from Universitas Indonesia and a Bangkit Academy Distinction Graduate, certified in machine learning by Google, GoTo, and Traveloka. My work sits at the intersection of computational science and visual storytelling.

On the technical side, I've shipped projects spanning medical imaging AI (Grad-CAM, CLAHE), physics-informed neural networks for rainfall forecasting, and interactive data dashboards. I work primarily in Python with TensorFlow, OpenCV, and the PyData stack.

Away from code, I work as a Director of Photography — lighting events and crafting films in the Adobe Creative Cloud environment. I believe the precision required in physics and the intuition required in filmmaking aren't so different.
